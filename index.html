Geet Music Player

Select a song to play

How to use

  • Press the settings button to open sidebar
  • Click on Add Songs and Select the songs to play
This App is serverless i.e. the Song you select do not get uploded to any server, instead it is still there in the browser as a blob file, which gets deleted as soon as you refresh the window.
